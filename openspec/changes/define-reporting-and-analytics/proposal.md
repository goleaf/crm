# Proposal: define-reporting-and-analytics

## Change ID
- `define-reporting-and-analytics`

## Summary
- Deliver workspace-level reporting that covers sales, leads, deals, and activity with exportable outputs and embeddable dashboard widgets.
- Provide a custom report builder that supports filters, grouping, aggregations, date ranges, and saved views with sharing controls.
- Ensure reports are exportable (CSV/XLSX/PDF) and can be surfaced as dashboard widgets with refresh intervals.

## Capabilities
- `sales-and-lead-reports`: Standard sales and lead reports with filters, grouping, and time bucketing.
- `pipeline-and-activity-reports`: Deal pipeline snapshots and activity productivity views with stage/owner filters.
- `custom-builder-and-export`: Self-serve report builder, saved reports, exports, and dashboard widgets.

## Notes
- OpenSpec CLI tooling is unavailable in this environment; specifications are drafted manually.
